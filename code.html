Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance do I need to book? ▼

We require a minimum of 12 hours advance notice for all bookings. For day tours, multi-stop journeys, and vehicle hire packages, we recommend 24–48 hours ahead. Last-minute requests may be accommodated — contact us via WhatsApp to check.

Can I book an airport transfer for an early morning or late-night flight? ▼

Yes. We operate 24/7 and cover all flight times including early departures and late-night arrivals. We track all flights and adjust pickup times automatically if your flight is delayed. No extra charge for flight delays.

Can I customise a day tour itinerary? ▼

Absolutely. Every tour is fully private and tailored to your group. Tell us your interests — history, whisky, wildlife, golf, or castles — and we'll build the perfect itinerary. We know Scotland, and we love creating experiences.

Do you cover multi-stop journeys? ▼

Yes. Multi-stop journeys are one of our most popular services. Whether it's distillery hopping, a castle route, or a business day with multiple meetings — we handle the full itinerary from a single booking at one fixed price.

How do I book via WhatsApp? ▼

Simply message us on WhatsApp at +44 7469 598219. Tell us your journey details and we'll send your fixed price quote within minutes. It's the fastest way to get a confirmed booking.
