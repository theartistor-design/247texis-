Frequently Asked Questions

Do you track flights? add

Yes, we monitor all flights in real-time using live tracking data. If your flight is delayed or arrives early, your driver will automatically adjust their arrival time at no extra cost.

Are your prices fixed with no hidden charges? add

Absolutely. The price we quote you is the final price you pay. This includes all airport fees, tolls, and taxes—there are never any hidden surprises.

Can I pre-book in advance? add

We highly recommend pre-booking as early as possible to guarantee availability, especially during peak travel seasons. You can book months in advance or at short notice.

Do you cover all of Scotland? add

Yes, while we have popular routes, we provide transfers to and from any location in Scotland, including all major cities, airports, and remote Highland destinations.

What payment methods do you accept? add

We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as cash. For your convenience, we can also arrange secure payment links or corporate account invoicing.

Do you offer meet and greet service? add

Yes, our standard airport service includes a professional meet and greet in the arrivals hall. Your driver will be waiting with a name board to assist you.
