Wedding Hire Scotland

Premium Transport to and from Your Wedding Venue

Arrive in style. Leave in comfort. Professional private hire for wedding guests, families and wedding parties across Scotland.

Get Instant Quote

Attending a wedding is a special occasion and your transport should reflect that. Scotland 247 Taxis provides premium private hire for wedding guests, family members, and wedding parties who need reliable, comfortable, and stylish transport to and from wedding venues across Scotland. Whether you are travelling to a castle in the Highlands, a hotel in Edinburgh, or a country estate in Perthshire, we will get you there in comfort and on time.

Our wedding hire service is simple — you choose your vehicle, tell us your pickup location, destination, and time, and we confirm your fixed price booking via WhatsApp. All bookings require a minimum of 12 hours advance notice. We recommend booking as early as possible for peak wedding season dates — particularly summer Saturdays — to guarantee availability of your preferred vehicle.

Scotland 247 Taxis offers three premium vehicles specifically for wedding hire bookings — the Audi RS7, Range Rover, and Range Rover 7-Seater for larger groups. All vehicles are immaculately maintained, professionally driven by fully licensed drivers, and available at fixed prices confirmed before your journey begins. No hidden charges, no surprises — just premium transport for a special occasion.
