Premium Service Guaranteed

There are journeys where a fixed transfer from A to B is simply not enough. A day of business meetings across three cities. A Highland exploration with no fixed itinerary. A corporate event where your clients need to move between venues seamlessly all day long. For these occasions, Scotland 247 Taxis provides a dedicated private driver service — one professional chauffeur, one luxury vehicle, entirely at your disposal for as many hours as you need.

Your private driver remains with you throughout your booking. There are no concerns about being left stranded, no app to reload, no surge pricing when demand increases. Your driver is a professional who understands confidentiality, punctuality, and the art of being present without being intrusive. Whether you need to make calls in the back seat without concern, or simply want to absorb Scotland's scenery in silence, your driver adapts entirely to your needs.

Our private driver hire is available from a minimum of 4 hours, with packages extending to full day, overnight, and multi-day hire for extended Scotland exploration or corporate travel programmes. All vehicles are maintained to the highest standard of cleanliness and comfort. Complimentary water is provided as standard. Wi-Fi is available on request. And every booking is fixed price — confirmed before your journey begins with nothing added afterwards.
