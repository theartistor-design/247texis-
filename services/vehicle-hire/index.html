Flexible Vehicle Hire Scotland
Book by the Hour or by the Week

Total control over your schedule and your journey. From 4 hours to 7 days — our hire packages give you Scotland on your own terms.

Get Instant Quote

Premium Service Guaranteed

Not every journey fits neatly into a single point-to-point transfer. Sometimes you need a vehicle and a professional driver at your disposal for an entire morning, a full working day, or even an entire week across Scotland. Our flexible vehicle hire packages are designed precisely for these situations — giving you the freedom of a private vehicle without the complexity of self-drive in unfamiliar Scottish roads and conditions.

Scotland's roads can be challenging for visitors unfamiliar with single-track Highland routes, passing places, and rapidly changing weather conditions. Having an experienced local driver behind the wheel means you can focus entirely on the experience — absorbing the scenery, attending to business, or simply relaxing — while your driver handles the route, the navigation, and the vehicle.

All hire packages include a dedicated professional driver, your choice of vehicle from our fleet, complimentary water, and a fixed price confirmed before your journey begins. We offer packages from 4 hours to 7 days, with custom arrangements available for longer corporate engagements or extended Scotland exploration.

What Is Included

We provide premium amenities and travel features as standard inside all packages.

Your Choice of Vehicle

Select any vehicle from our full fleet — Standard, Executive, or Prestige class. Your driver comes with the vehicle as part of every hire package.

Fixed Hourly Rate

Our hire rate is fixed at booking. No meter, no surge, no overtime penalties for running slightly long. What we quote is what you pay.

Minimum 4 Hours

All flexible hire packages start from a minimum of 4 hours. Perfect for half-day city exploration, airport plus meetings, or a short Highland excursion.

Multi-Stop Flexibility

Stop wherever you choose, for however long you need. Your driver waits at every stop. Perfect for business days with multiple meetings or tourism with no fixed schedule.

Corporate Packages Available

Regular corporate clients can arrange account-based hire with centralised booking, monthly invoicing, and priority vehicle availability.

Complimentary Refreshments

Still and sparkling water provided as standard in every vehicle. Champagne, soft drinks, and specific refreshments available on advance request.

Exclusive Experience

Designed For Chauffeur Travel

Every vehicle in our fleet is maintained in showroom condition, featuring plush leather seats, quiet cabins, climate control, and on-board chargers for a completely relaxing ride.
