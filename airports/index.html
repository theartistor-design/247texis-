Scottish Airport Transfers

Fixed price. Flight tracked. Professional meet and greet at every major Scottish airport.

Book Your Airport Transfer

Scotland's airports are the gateway to one of the world's most breathtaking destinations. Whether you are arriving for business, embarking on a Highland adventure, or beginning a whisky tour across Speyside, Scotland 247 Taxis ensures your journey from the aircraft to your destination is as seamless and comfortable as possible. We cover every major Scottish airport with the same professional standard of service — fixed pricing, real-time flight tracking, and professional meet and greet in the arrivals hall.

Every airport transfer we complete includes real-time flight monitoring from the moment your flight departs. If your flight is delayed, diverted, or arrives early, your driver already knows and adjusts automatically. You will never need to call us or send a message. We watch every flight, every time, at no extra charge. Our fixed pricing means the fare you are quoted is the fare you pay — regardless of traffic, time of day, or how long the airport drop-off queue takes.

Scotland 247 Taxis serves all five major Scottish airports — Edinburgh, Glasgow, Aberdeen, Inverness, and Glasgow Prestwick — with professional licensed drivers who hold full private hire licensing and are background checked to the highest standard. Whether your journey takes you to the heart of Edinburgh, the remote Scottish Highlands, or anywhere in between, we will get you there safely, comfortably, and on time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you track flights automatically?

expand_more
Yes — we monitor every inbound flight in real time from departure. If your flight is delayed, early, or diverted, your driver adjusts automatically. You never need to contact us about flight changes.

Is meet and greet included?

expand_more
Yes — meet and greet inside the arrivals hall with a personalised name board is included in every airport transfer at no extra charge.

What if my flight is cancelled?

expand_more
Contact us via WhatsApp or email as soon as you know about a cancellation and we will rearrange your transfer. We understand flight disruptions are outside your control.

Do you cover all Scottish airports?

expand_more
Yes — Edinburgh (EDI), Glasgow (GLA), Aberdeen (ABZ), Inverness (INV), and Glasgow Prestwick (PIK). Contact us for transfers to or from any of these airports.

How far in advance should I book?

expand_more
We require a minimum of 12 hours advance notice for all bookings. For busy travel periods we recommend booking as early as possible to guarantee availability.
